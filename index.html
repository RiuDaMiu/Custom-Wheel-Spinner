Custom Wheel Spinner

By: Ria Patel























Directions

This app is designed to intake all of your different options into account and make a random decision. There are up to 6 slots on the wheel in which can be uploaded from the area to the right. Click the -Put Them on the Wheel!- button to add the different options to the wheel. Then click the -Spin- button to spin the wheel and discover the winning option. Good Luck!


Tips and Tricks:

1. If you have 2 or 3 options, repeat them in order to reach the end.
2. If there are 4 or 5 options, meaning there are 1 or 2 empty slots, treat them as a -spin again- area, or simply name them -spin again-.
3. Please keep in mind the size of the text when inputed, abbreviation is recommended.
